Interactive · Where the recall actually lives

A page of text, chunked at whatever size you pick. Every question in the top row is a real query. The heatmap shows recall @ 5 on each query as chunk size varies. There is no universally right size.

How this demo works

The three curves are stylized recall@5 profiles for three query types we saw in the paper — factoid ("what year was X?"), definitional ("what is Y?"), and multi-hop ("how does X relate to Y?"). Their optima sit at different chunk sizes, which is exactly why "just pick 512" is bad advice. The vertical marker follows your slider.
