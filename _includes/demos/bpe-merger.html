What's happening: the algorithm scans every adjacent pair in the corpus, finds the highest-frequency one, replaces every occurrence with a new token, and adds that token to the vocabulary. After enough merges, common substrings (th, the, cat) become single tokens; rare strings stay multi-piece. That's GPT-2's tokenizer in miniature.
