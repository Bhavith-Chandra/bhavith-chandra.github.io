This demo lets you poke at all 72 of distilGPT2's heads (6 layers × 12 heads) on a real sentence and see the attention weights they actually use.

How attention heads get characterised

Each head's attention matrix is a pattern. Some patterns are generic (attend to the previous token; attend to the BOS token). Some are specific (attend to the previous occurrence of the current token — the signature of an induction head). In this demo we compute a quick diagnosis: "previous-token" if the matrix has most mass on the diagonal-minus-one; "BOS sink" if most mass sits on position 0; "self" if it's almost pure self-attention; otherwise we call it "mixed." This is not the full machinery of Olsson et al. 2022, but it gives you a feel for the different regimes.
