How this demo works

The curves are smooth analytical fits to the "lost in the middle" pattern observed in the literature and reproduced in our paper. The baseline curve peaks at the start and end of the context; the fine-tuned curve is the same shape at 0% fine-tuning, and progressively flattens toward uniform accuracy as the slider moves. Clicking a depth bucket shows a stylized model completion at that position — correct on the fine-tuned side, hallucinated on the baseline side when the depth is unfavorable.
