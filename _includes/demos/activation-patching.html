Mini · Activation patching, find where the answer lives

Two prompts disagree about the answer. Run both. Then copy the residual stream from the corrupt run into a single layer of the clean run, the patch, and watch the prediction shift. The layers where patching flips the answer are the layers where the answer-relevant computation happens.

clean Paris is the capital of → France
corrupt Tokyo is the capital of → Japan
click a layer to patch it from the corrupt run into the clean run:
after patching layer —:
P(France)
87%
P(Japan)
1%

What the curve tells you: patching very early layers does almost nothing; the model hasn't yet computed anything answer-specific. Patching very late layers does almost nothing; the answer has already been extracted. The big effect lives in the middle, exactly where MLP-based factual recall happens. This is the technique Meng et al. used to discover ROME.
