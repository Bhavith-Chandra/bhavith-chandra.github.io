How this demo works

The character positions are perturbed by a synthetic elastic field whose amplitude you control. The "confidence" curve is a stylized fit to what we actually observed: the un-augmented model drops off a cliff around amplitude 0.4; the augmented model degrades gracefully out to about 0.8, matching the paper's numbers within a few points.
